About me

My name is Cesar Gonzalez, and I am a software developer with 4 years of professional experience building enterprise software. I have experience working with mobile communication systems, medical research software and videogame development.

The programming languages and environments I am most proficient with are C, C++ and Python, but I also have experience working with JavaScript and C#. I am used to do development on Linux. I am also experienced with using Windows and macOS as development environments when it is needed.

Besides my professional experience, I am passionate about software crafting and I love to learn in my free time. In my Github profile you can see some of the projects I have been working on, the latest one being Cest, a C++ testing framework focused on simplicity and readability.
