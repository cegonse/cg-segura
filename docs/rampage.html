Rampage of Technology (LudumDare '36)

A mad hipster wants to be the biggest hipster ever and YOU are the next victim! You will play as a cassette, a discman or the legendary Nokia 3310 to run away from the hipster and save your own life! This is the RAMPAGE OF TECHNOLOGY!

This game was made for the Ludum Dare 36 in the GameBoss Jam held in Zaragoza, Spain.

Game link: https://natnatart.itch.io/rampage-of-technology
