Tower of Elements



Tower of Elements is a puzzle-platformer that defies you with logic games to overcome the obstacles of this misterious tower. Control the four elements and get to the top!

As the tradition says in the Mage Clan, newcomers must show their skills, intelligence and dexerity getting into the Tower of Elements and getting through its obstacles. You will master air, water, fire and earth on your way to the top. You will challenge many dangers and the creatures that lure inside the tower. Will you be worthy of joining the clan?

Through your aventure you will encounter four magic powers. Ice, which will let you create ice blocks to reach high places. Fire, to melt ice blocks blocking your way. Wind, to move ice blocks that are on the map. And earth, which will create unbreakable blocks to get to the hardest places.
