Galaxy Survivor



In Galaxy Survivor you will explore the galaxy, upgrade your ship with awesome skills, avoid the asteroid fields and get all the stars. Upgrade your ship with skills like:

  • Boost your speed.
  • Bombs to destroy the asteroid fields.
  • Repair yourself from damages.
  • And many more.
Get to the end of the galaxy, and share your best score with your friends. Challenge them!
