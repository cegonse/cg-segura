Capture the Pixel

Capture the Pixel is a fast-paced game featuring online multiplayer combat for up to 32 players simultaneously in the same room! Fight in a 3D moba map, learn the sweet spots, and defeat your digital enemies alone or with your friends.

Use more than 6 different weapons (more to come in updates) to defend yourself and your friends. Gather your friends and play together or versus each other while you help build the game with your opinions and suggestions in the next updates.
